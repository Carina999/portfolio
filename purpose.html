Purpose of our project
Start Share Diary Purpose Artists Advice
Pandemic and lockdowns of this scale have been an event unseen in human history. During lockdowns, people will generate different feelings that are completely different from normal life.

They would have an entirely different mindset due to the different ways of interacting with the rest of the world – for instance, the online classes or work and blocked space. People’s mentality and behavior during lockdown might be unique, but how could we share them with other people? To the next generation? So our project is targeted at everyone who experienced the lockdown of this pandemic and the future generations.
